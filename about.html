Suad's Story

Early life

Suad was born and raised in the capital city of Accra. Gravitating towards the arts, reading and writing stories became a common habit growing from a means of self expression into a way to connect with other people. This passion coupled with a firm sense of justice eventually led her to seek a career in law.

Corporate career

During law school, she started her first business, Lustre Labs. Formerly known as Shea Lustre, the artisanal skincare company was ideated during a causal stroll in school in anticipation of an annual goods market. She saw the opportunity to fill what seemed to be a growing demand for affordable and accessible organic skincare products in her community. After 4 years of hard work behind the scenes, Lustre Labs officially became a recognised legal entity in 2021. After law school , Suad entered the high powered corporate world working as a legal aid for the renowned Ghanaian subsidiary of Tullow Oil . After a year, she devoted her talents to the Legal tech startup Judy Innovative Technologies first as a legal content acquisition associate before pivoting into a more technical role.

TODAY

As Lustre Labs still operates, Suad is looking to expand her portfolio into retail and software entreprenership. To strengthen her already existing skills and connect with a network of like-minded people, she now holds a coveted spot as 1 of 51 Entrepreneurs from 19 African countries at MEST Africa, where she is studying Business, Technology and Marketing & Communications.
